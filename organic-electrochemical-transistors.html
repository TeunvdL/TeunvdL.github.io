Organic Electrochemical Transistors for Physical Neural Networks

Project Duration: April â€“ July 2023

Institute: Institute for Complex Molecular Systems, TU/e

This project focused on investigating organic electrochemical transistors as components for physical neural networks. The work was a collaborative effort, combining hardware development with computational modeling. Key activities included:

  • Fabricating chips in a cleanroom environment.
  • Analyzing the stability and adjustability of the transistors.
  • Modeling their behavior within a neural network simulation.

The project offered a unique blend of hardware development and computational simulation, and culminated in a comprehensive report. The experience provided valuable insights into the intersection of molecular electronics and neural networks.
